Combinatorial creativity combines existing concepts in a novel way in order to produce new concepts

In this paper we concentrate on creating new concepts in the description logic EL
we propose a novel language to this effect
we study a novel language properties and complexity
we show that we language can be used to model existing inventions and  to a limited degree  to generate new concepts
